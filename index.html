About Olga Sokolov

Olga Sokolov is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP-BC) with extensive experience in geriatric care. With her comprehensive nursing education and years of dedicated practice, Olga provides compassionate, evidence-based healthcare services tailored to the unique needs of older adults.

Her expertise in geriatric care ensures that patients receive specialized attention for age-related health concerns, promoting both quality of life and independence.

Services Offered

Managing Chronic Conditions

Comprehensive care for diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, arthritis, and other chronic health conditions common in older adults

Dementia Management

Specialized care and support for patients with dementia and their families, including cognitive assessments and care planning

Preventative Care

Health screenings, vaccinations, wellness visits, and lifestyle counseling to maintain optimal health and prevent disease

Medication Management

Review and optimization of medication regimens to ensure safety and effectiveness

Care Coordination

Collaboration with specialists (such as physical therapists, occupational therapists, and psychiatrists) and healthcare teams to provide comprehensive patient care

Home Visits & Telehealth

Convenient in-home consultations and virtual appointments for patients with mobility challenges or transportation difficulties
