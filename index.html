About

Olga Sokolov, FNP-BC

Olga is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner dedicated to serving the elderly community of Northern Virginia with personalized medical care and comprehensive support. She focuses on patients in assisted living facilities, understanding the unique healthcare needs that arise in these settings, and works closely with facility staff to ensure seamless care coordination.

Her mission is high-quality care that enhances the health and well-being of older adults — allowing them to live with dignity and independence — while supporting caregivers and facility teams through every stage of care.
