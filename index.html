About Gericare and Olga Sokolov

Gericare, as Olga's practice is dedicated to serving the elderly community of Northern Virginia with personalized medical care and comprehensive support services. It is led by Olga Sokolov, a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP-BC) with a passion for geriatric healthcare and a focus on serving patients in assisted living facilities, understanding the unique healthcare needs and challenges that arise in these settings. She works closely with facility staff to ensure seamless care coordination and provides on-site visits to deliver personalized medical attention to residents who may have difficulty accessing traditional healthcare settings.

Aging is a journey that deserves compassionate, specialized care tailored to each individual's unique needs and circumstances. Our mission is to provide high-quality, care that enhances the health and well-being of older adults, allowing them to live fulfilling lives with dignity and independence. Our services support both seniors and their caregivers throughout all stages of health, from routine wellness care to complex medical management and recovery. Gericare also provides education and community resources designed to enhance overall health outcomes and quality of life for our senior patients.
